# Content Growth Playbook (YouTube/X/TikTok)

## 四周迭代框架
- Week 1: Hot Topics Engine（每天抓10个候选）
- Week 2: 模板化（3个短视频模板）
- Week 3: A/B（开场文案、封面标题）
- Week 4: 变现验证（引流/社群/服务）

## 选题评分字段（Google Sheets）
Date | Platform | Keyword | Views | Comments | Publish Date | 7d Views/Subs | Comment Rate | Freshness Score | Total Score | Decision

### 建议公式
- Comment Rate = Comments / Views
- Freshness Score = max(0, 10 - days_since_publish)
- Total Score = 0.5*(7d Views/Subs) + 0.3*(Comment Rate*200) + 0.2*Freshness
